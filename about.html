About Malick Kasse /

Welcome to MalicksEyes & AnnaH , Enhance your skills with best online tutorials and travels experiences.

Hello, I'm Malick KASSE, a proud representative of Mali, a land adorned with vibrant cultures and breathtaking landscapes. My journey into the realm of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) began during a profound moment at the GDG Devfest in Bamako, Mali. It was there, in the midst of innovative minds and cutting-edge technology at the fablab, that I found more than just machinery – I found a mentor whose guidance set my heart ablaze with a fervor for engineering. Joining Mali's national robotics team, "Team makers," was a dream turned reality. Together, we soared to new heights, clinching the 2nd place at the PanAfrican Robotics Competition in Accra, Ghana. The rush of pride and camaraderie in achieving this milestone expanded my worldview beyond borders, touching the depths of my soul. Bearing witness to the power of innovation, I stood as a speaker at events like the Next Einstein Forum, sharing our vision for a Smart City. When our dedication caught the eye of President Ibrahim Boubacar Keita, who honored us with laptops, it wasn't just a gift – it was a symbol of recognition for our relentless pursuit of knowledge. Fueled by an unyielding passion for technology, I embarked on a journey through YouTube, crafting a channel in French to spread the wonders of science and tech. This voyage of sharing knowledge led to an opportunity to mentor Team makers, guiding them to victory in Dakar, Senegal. In 2022, leaving behind the familiar, I ventured into India. Amidst the unknown, the warmth of my passion for Electronics and Communication Engineering and the echoes of my YouTube endeavors kept my spirit alight. Launching a second channel became my canvas to paint the unseen beauty of India and narrate the tales of fellow international students. In the midst of workshops, IoT explorations, and writing, my journey is an emotional odyssey - an exploration of human connection, cultural diversity, and the unwavering pursuit of knowledge. Come, join me through "malickseyes" as we delve into a world where emotions transcend boundaries - welcome to Malick's eyes!
